Mom's Egg Rolls

(Makes ~50 rolls; time 24 hours)

Ingredients

Cilantro1/2 Cup (leaves)
Carrots1 1/2 Cup (finely chopped)
Onion1 (red, medium, diced)
Pork Loin2 1/2 lbs
Egg1 large
Egg White1 large
Soy Sauce1/2 Cup
Lumpia Wrapper

Equipment

Meat Grinder
Deep Fryer

Preparation

Cut pork loin into 1" cubes. Use meat grinder on pork. Whisk egg and soy sauce together. Set aside. Mix cilantro, carrots, onions and pork. Use meat grinder on mixture. Mix soy sauce and egg mixture with meat grinder mixture. Place in a container, seal, and set aside overnight.

Place 2 lumpia wrappers on a flat surface. Place a tablespoon of the meat mixture on the wrapper. Wrap the meat up. Coat the wrapper with egg white to seal the contents of the egg roll in place.

Cooking

Heat oil until boiling.

Place egg rolls in in deep fryer. Keep in until golden brown. Egg rolls are cooked when they begin to float.

Notes:

If meat grinder is inaccessible ground pork can be purchased and a food processor (or man power) can be used to finely chop vegetables before mixing ingredients together.

The video (click second paragraph in preparations section) roughly shows how to wrap your lumpia. They use an egg mixture, but Nanay recommends using just egg whites.
